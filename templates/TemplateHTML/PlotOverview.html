Summary

Boundary of Shade and Future follows Selene von Anima; a young Mage from Regnella who, according to her people's customs, devotes her life to fighting monsters and fixing magical anomalies across the land in return for honour and wealth.
After a strange force starts corrupting Regnella and her people however, Selene ends up cursed by the perpetrator; losing her mind and will to the very monster she is fighting, and the only way to break this curse is to kill its origin: the ancient and wicked Queen of Time.
-However, the witch-queen cannot be slain by ordinary means, leaving the young Mage's only hope to be the mystic Gifts of Arushi...
-But even the Gifts of Light are said to contain a dark power, and what of the legendary blade Dainsleif?
