Welcome to Regnella Wiki!

This is the official wiki for Boundary of Shade and Future, launched on the 18/11/2024 to provide long-time fans and newcomers alike with interesting facts and information on the game.
Boundary of Shade and Future was released on PCs in 20/01/2021 and chronicles the journey of Selene von Anima and Takeo Akashiro in their quest to defeat the nefarious Queen of Time and break the curse on Regnella; their beloved homeland.
Whether you require the stats for a particularly annoying boss, the romance options for one of the party members, or simply want to chat about the game, you have come to the right place.

Synopsis

In the kingdom of Regnella, those with magic are granted the greatest honour and privileges in return for their service to the Crown and kingdom all their lives...
One such Mage; Selene von Anima is tasked with investigating the cause of a curse placed over her homeland; one that corrupts all who it touches, turning them into nigh-immortal monsters.
-However, upon coming face-to-face with the perpetrator; a powerful and ancient Witch who calls herself the 'Queen of Time', Selene loses everything; friends, magic, and nearly her own mind.
Cursed by the monster she was sent to kill, Selene and her mysterious new ally Takeo Akashiro; a defected knight serving the witch-queen, must race against the clock to break the curse and defeat the sorceress before Selene losses her humanity entirety; and with it, the last hope of Regnella...
